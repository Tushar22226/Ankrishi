About Ankrishi

Our Mission

Ankrishi is a hackathon project with a mission to explore how technology can transform India's agricultural ecosystem by connecting farmers with buyers, providing information access, and offering financial management tools.

Our Concept

We envision sustainable, community-driven growth through a village-by-village scaling model that would provide communities with the support and training needed to maximize technology benefits.

Technology Stack

Built with React Native and Firebase, our prototype demonstrates how mobile platforms can work efficiently even in areas with limited connectivity, making technology accessible to rural communities.

Community-Centered

Local ambassadors and peer support networks

Mobile-First

Optimized for smartphones with offline capabilities

Multilingual

Support for local languages and dialects

Secure & Trusted

Verification systems and secure transactions
